Ron Cherny

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo studying Pure Mathematics and Combinatorics.

In Spring 2023, I worked under Matthew Satriano together with Yohan Song on a combinatorial instance of the Gerstenhaber problem.

In Winter 2025, I worked under Oliver Pechenik together with Stephan Pfannerer, trying to understand the structure of promotion permutations.

In the upcoming Fall 2025, I will work under Matthew Kennedy.
